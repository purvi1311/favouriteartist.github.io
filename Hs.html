About

Harry styles was born on 1st February 1994 in Redditch,Worcestershire. He entered the music industry at the age of 14, and his careers started of when he was grouped with four other boys on the X-factor to form a band and were named One-direction later. They went on four tours and released 5 albums. After the band broke up in 2015, he went on with his solo career and released two albums: HS1 and Fine line. He followed these albums with two tours called Harry Styles: Live on tour and Love on Tour.
